contributing photos

If you'd like to submit a mirror selfie, you've got two options:

  1. Tag me (@mun_fases) in your photo on instagram (this is the easiest for me) orrrr
  2. Send me the image by direct message

some notes:

  • Your photo needs to include a reflection of you and your phone/camera in a mirror-like surface.
  • The more interesting the mirror, the more fun for me! There's an account at this time of writing with amaaazing mirror selfies, @mirrormirour, if you're looking for inspiration.
  • I've noticed that mirror selfies where the subject is off-center can sometimes make for more interesting moon collages.
  • I probably won't know ahead of time when your photo will be included -- you might see it in a couple days, or maybe in a couple of months.
  • If you have non-selfie images you'd like to contribute, I would love to see them. Send them to me via dm, or tag me in the photo (again, this way is easier for me), and let me know whether you're contributing them for me to use with any collage, or if you'd prefer that they only get paired with your mirror selfie.
